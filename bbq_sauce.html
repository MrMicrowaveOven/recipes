Fatty BBQ Sauce (Mayo-based)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 2 tsp Yellow Mustard
  • 2 tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1/2 tsp hot sauce
  • 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ teaspoon Onion Powder
  • ½ teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • ½ teaspoon Paprika
  • Pinch Cayenne
  • ½ teaspoon Black Pepper
  • ½ teaspoon Kosher Salt
  • Steak Seasoning (1 tbsp or so)
  • Sugar Free BBQ Sauce (3 tbsp or so)

Directions

Mix all ingredients MINUS the BBQ Sauce and Steak Seasoning. It'll be a white-ish sauce, and taste almost like a savory BBQ sauce. Add BBQ Sauce and Steak Seasoning to taste. Generally 1 tbsp Steak Seasoning and 3 tbsp of BBQ sauce works for me. It makes it taste like genuine BBQ sauce to me, though you might need to add more.

My BBQ sauce is G Hughes Sugar Free, which is 1 carb per tablespoon. Since the goal is to make this as fatty as possible, I try to add a minimum amount of BBQ sauce, which is just empty carbs.
